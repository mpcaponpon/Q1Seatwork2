Characters of Noli Me Tangere

Image Name of the Character Description
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin Crisostomo born after his family's name, "Ibarra". He lived with a wealthy family in San Diego, he became friends with Maria Clara which made them Childhood friends. He left his place to study in Europe in 1874.
Maria Clara de los Santos Maria Clara, daughter of Padre Damaso, and Fiance of Crisostomo Ibarra. Maria grew up with Kapitan Tiago due to her mother's death after her birth. She is Ibarra's childhood friend, she studied in Sta. Clara.
Tenyente Guevarra Tenyente Guevarra, full name is Miguel Guevarra. He is an old lieutenant of the Guardia CIvil, meaning Civil Guards. He is a friend of Don Rafael Ibarra.
Don Rafael Ibarra Crisostomo's Father, he died before the book started. And since he is a close friend of Tenyente Guevarra, he told Crisostomo about his father back then.
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso, full name is Damaso Verdolagas. A Spanish priest in the Philippines during 1858. He was a priest for three years in San Diego.
